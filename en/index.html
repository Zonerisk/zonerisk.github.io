Risk Management

ZONERISK is a Web tool that supports the project manager in managing project risk.From identification to monitoring and risk control, this tool improves the quality of information to communicate.

Other features include:
- Outstanding issues and problems,
- Multi-Level Structure,
- Performance Measurement;

Efficiency

Do not waste time consolidating information, but spend it on making good decisions. Whether its by the quality of our reports or the accuracy of our charts, our goal is to give you the tools you need.

Integrated Solution

The proverb "The whole is greater than the sum of its parts" aptly summarizes the value of an integrated system. Stop searching for your documents, all the information you need is centralized in way that everything can be reach within a few clicks.

Framework

By combining the processes of PMI's PMBoK and SEI's CMMI, you get the best industry practices in terms of project management. In addition, we add our experience in project management to ensure that everything is practical.

Usability

After your first contact you will find yourself confortable with the user interface. Each screen is carefully designed so as to encourage interaction and allow the user to achieve his objective.
